Recommended Job Sites:

Indeed
Good for any type of job.
CareerBuilder
Similar to Indeed or Monster, good for all job types.
Monster
Much like CareerBuilder or Indeed, another good site for most job types.
Dice
Primarily for IT or Programming Roles.
Glassdoor
Good for company reviews and for applying to jobs of all types.
Hired
A bit like Dice, you submit your resume and profile and Hired tries to match you with jobs in your area. Companies apply to you, and you sort throught the requests for the job you want.
Angel
Good site for joining the team of Start-Ups in your area
ZipRecruiter
This is another alternative to sites like Indeed or Monster. Good for all job types.
